Shelby Drabant

Not a hero or a legend, just a humble man making a tribute page of himself.
Shelby Drabant, not depicted in the picture above, would not be born until decades after this photo was taken.

    Here's a time line of Shelby Drabant through the lense of video game technological releases:

  • 1988 - Born in Las Vegas, Nevada (Sega Genisis also releases)
  • 1993 - Introduced to the Atari 2600 and the original Nintendo gaming consoles, Shelby began developing an afinity to video games and technology.
  • 1995 - Parents bring a Macintosh Performa 5200 home and inspire a new era into Shelby's life. With games like Power Pete and Battle Chess, there was no end in sight for entertainment.
  • 1999 - Sega Dreamcast comes out on young Shelby's birthday. Many say this was a turning point towards a darker path of time wasted.
  • 2000 - Building his first desktop, Shelby began his journey into the amazing world of PCs.
  • 2001 - Microsoft enters the fray with the Xbox. Halo destroys Shelby's life and many other young adolescents.
  • 2002 - Shelby introduces a ATI Radeon 9700 Pro into his desktop and recieves a free copy of Half-Life and begins exploring its many mods.
  • 2003 - The Game Boy Advance SP is released and no console or PC can hold Shelby back from its versatility.
  • 2005 - Xbox 360, Wii and PlayStation 3 all come out bringing the consol war into full effect. Shelby drifts away from the console wars as Steam improves and he builds a better and faster rig.
  • 2007 - Shelby graduates from High School with high honors! Mostly because he didn't want to wear the school colors which were brown and yellow. High honors got to wear white.
  • 2008 - Shelby attends UNLV and begins to study Biology, although maintains a major interest in computer science.
  • 2013 - Being so close to finishing a B.S. in Biology, Shelby finishes his degree and quickly shifts gears to his true passion and begins taking Computer Science courses.
  • 2016 - Shelby moves to Seattle Washington to attend UW to further his education in Software Engineering.
  • 2017 - Shelby finishes and intense computer program and begins his life as a Software Engineer. Although many console have arisen during this, all are ignored for the glory of the PC.
